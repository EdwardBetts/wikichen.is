My (real) name is Jonathan E. Chen. I go by wikichen on the web. I can't exactly say what I am—defined as a member of society, not as an entity in the universe, in which case I would be a homo sapien, an animal, a collection of atoms, sentient stardust, and so forth—because, as fate would have it, I'm not exactly sure yet, so I've decided to collect a whole bunch of things I do here to make sense of my life and hope it leads me somewhere.

I haven't really thought out my little corner of the Internet, but as an exercise in the “shipped is better than perfect” school of thought, I'll add content first and make improvements over time; hopefully, my dear visitor, you'll appreciate being taken along for the ride. After all, as with many adventures in life, it's often not about the destination, but rather the journey itself.

Right now, I'm—

  • writing to become a better person
  • reading as a matter of personal principle
  • telling stories (work in progress!)

Who knows, maybe I don't need to know what I am to the world to mean something to it.

Cheers,
